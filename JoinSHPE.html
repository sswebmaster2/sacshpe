Become a SHPE member!

Join us! We'd love to have you!

Be a part of the nation's largest association dedicated to fostering Hispanic leadership in the STEM field.

Skillsets

Earn at least 3 skillsets from different categories below.

Skillsets

Earning skillsets must be done throughout the semester. Earn at least 3 in total, and each must be earned from different categories.
Academics

- Attend an Academic Event
- Attend four Study Sessions
- Participate in the Academics Committee

Chapter Development

- Attend a SHPE Fundraiser
- Attend a Social Event
- Participate in the Events Committee
- Participate in the Fundraising Committee

Community Service

- Volunteer at an event recognized by SHPE

Leadership

- Attend a Leadership Event
- Attend a Conference
- Earn the Star Membership Award

Outreach

- Attend an Outreach Event
- Participate in the Outreach Committee

Professional Development

- Attend a Professional
- Development Event
- Attend a Career Fair
- Participate in the Professional Development Committee

Mission
  • SHPE changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support, and development.
Vision
  • SHPE’s vision is a world where Hispanics are highly valued and influential as the leading innovators, scientists, mathematicians, and engineers.
